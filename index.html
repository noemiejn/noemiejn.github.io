Noémie Jeannin

PhD candidate at PV-Lab, EPFL

My research focuses on the use of electric vehicle batteries as short-term storage for solar photovoltaic electricity, also known as vehicle to grid (V2G).

More details

Current project

I am involved in the development of the citiwatts tool, an online geographical platform for energy planning. I am implementing the models developed in my research as calculation modules to make them accessible to everyone.

The tool is available for all of Europe, don't hesitate to try out your charging scenario!
