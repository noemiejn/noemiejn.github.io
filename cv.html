Noémie Jeannin

PhD candidate in Energy Systems

As final year PhD candidate at PVlab, EPFL, my research focuses on coupling photovoltaic production with electric vehicle charging. I am developing a methodology to quantify the vehicle charging needs using a geographical approach. My PhD is part of the European project OPENGIS4ET, which will result in an online geographic platform to support the energy transition: citiwatts.
